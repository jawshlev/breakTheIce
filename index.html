Gesture-Based Interactive Experience

Description

This is an interactive p5.js experience with ml5.js HandPose detection. The animation shows colorful circles that move in a wave pattern and respond to your hand gestures through your webcam.

You can interact with the canvas by moving your mouse, clicking, or performing a pointing gesture with your index finger. Use the controls at the top to pause/play the animation or reset it.

Controls

  • Mouse Movement: Move your cursor over the canvas to interact with elements
  • Click: Create a new colored circle at the cursor position
  • Play/Pause Button: Toggle the animation
  • Reset Button: Restart the experience

About

This interactive experience was created using p5.js and ml5.js with HandPose. p5.js is a JavaScript library that makes coding accessible for artists, designers, educators, and beginners.

ml5.js is a user-friendly machine learning library built on top of TensorFlow.js, and HandPose is a hand detection model that can track hand landmarks in real-time. Learn more at ml5js.org.

Gesture Controls

This experience uses your webcam to detect the following gestures:

  • Pointing Index Finger: Raise your index finger while keeping other fingers folded to trigger an effect
  • Mouse Interactions: Use your mouse to interact with the canvas directly

Note: For best results, ensure good lighting, face the camera directly, and position your hand clearly in the frame.
